Welcome to Jackie's Tropical Village in Malindi. The resort has been built according to kenyan tradition but features all modern comforts. An oasis of tranquillity, the resort is made up of 51 rooms located in the luscious tropical garden. A large beach with facilities, 2 restaurants and 3 bars complete the scene. Let the sound of the ocean gently cuddle you and be amazed every day by the wonderful colors of the sunsets that make Kenya a place famous and unique in the world. We don’t leave anything to chance and the resort’s all inclusive package is unparalleled in Malindi.
