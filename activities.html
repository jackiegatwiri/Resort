LIGHTING THE CHRISTMAS TREE

LET CHRISMAS TIME BEGIN !

Discover the unique spirit of Christmas at the Sandies Tropical Village and celebrate with us the beginning of this exceptional period of the year, starting with the lighting of the Christmas Tree in this wonderful place under the palm trees on a wonderful white sandy beach.

The Program includes

  • Serving of eggnog, glue wine & drinks
  • Cakes & traditional pudding
  • Cocktail party under the palm trees
  • Entrance of the father christmas from the beach with mail box to north pole
  • Receiving of kids christmas wish cards
